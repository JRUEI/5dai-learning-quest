💡 What You'll Learn

Today's whitepaper talks about the transition from manual syntax coding to intent-driven "vibe coding" and disciplined "agentic engineering."

"It explores how AI agents compress the traditional software development life cycle (SDLC)..."

...and explains the "factory model" where developers act as system orchestrators who design the evaluation, constraint, and context harnesses that safely guide autonomous execution.
